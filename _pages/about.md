Dr. Qirui Huang is Regional Head of Huawei's Central Research Institute in Singapore, bridging strategic technology planning and hands-on AI research. He leads a 30-person team driving Huawei's multimodal AI roadmap, while remaining deeply engaged in large model research spanning Transformers, Mamba, and Mixture-of-Experts architectures. He has authored 50+ peer-reviewed papers, holds 16 patents, and has secured USD 10M+ in research funding. He received his Ph.D. from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore.
