Bridging strategic technology planning and hands-on AI research, I lead a team driving the multimodal AI roadmap while remaining deeply engaged in large model research spanning Transformers, Mamba, and Mixture-of-Experts architectures. With extensive experience building large-scale training and inference systems — including GPU cluster orchestration, mixed-precision training, and high-throughput inference optimization — my work spans the full stack from research to production. I have authored 50+ peer-reviewed papers, hold 16 patents, and have secured USD 10M+ in research funding.
