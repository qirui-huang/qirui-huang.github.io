Towards Efficient Unified Multimodal Models: A Hybrid Mamba-Transformer Approach

We propose a 1.4B unified multimodal model that combines Mamba (SSM) and Transformer layers in a hybrid backbone with modal-aware Mixture-of-Experts routing, supporting both image understanding and text-to-image generation within a single autoregressive framework; our base model achieves 33.89% on MMMU (zero-shot), outperforming Chameleon-7B (33.78%), UniToken-7B (33.44%), and Emu3-8B (24.89%), while approaching DeepSeek Janus-Pro-1B (35.67%) — an instruction-tuned model — with only 0.36B active parameters per forward pass, demonstrating strong parameter efficiency through top-2 MoE routing across 8 experts.
